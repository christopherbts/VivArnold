Biography

Art has always been a source of fascination and wonder for me. These feelings I can trace to my childhood. It was from early on that I decided I would pursue the fine arts. From art classes in school I studied and completed a degree in Fine Art from the University of the Witwatersrand. Taking inspiration from the impressionists and those artists exploring the abstract realms of the imagination, I set to work forging my own identity within a medium that constantly looks to break convention. Since graduating I have exhibited my work at some of South Africaâ€™s best galleries. I continue to paint and learn to this day.
